Welcome to Open Book

What is this?


Hello everyone, this is an online library designed for everyone to allow easy access to different books. Anyone can access these books free of cost and enjoy them.



Designer


 This website has been desined by Yuvit  Batra, a student of AIS Noida, with the  objective to help everyone enjoy their  favourite books free of cost and help the less  privliged to get access to good quality  content.

Features of our website

This website has several Features to explore some of them are as follows:-

  1. Home: We our currently on the HomePage of the webite. This part tells us about the features of the page, the Designer, the purpose of this page etc.

  2. Browse: This is the part of the webapplication where you can see the different books available for renting.

  3. Login: This helps you login to our website and helps in allowing you to rent and provide books for renting.

  4. Remove: This allows you to remove a book that you had previously added.

  5. To Rent: Here you can upload books to share and help this small little community to grow.
